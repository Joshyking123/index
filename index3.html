The Jianguo Hotel Qianmen is located near Tiantan Park,
just a 10-minute walk from the National Center for the Performing
Arts and Tian'anmen Square. Built in 1956 it has old school
charm and many rooms still feature high, crown-molded ceilings.
A 2012 renovation brought all rooms and services up to modern
day scratch and guestrooms come equipped with free Wi-Fi and
all the usual amenities required for a comfortable stay.
